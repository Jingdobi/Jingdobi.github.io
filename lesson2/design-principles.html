Design Principles Document
Porter Merrell

Proximity
Dribbble.com

Dribbble (With three b's) is a designer website, where graphic and web designers can congregate and show off their work and passion projects. The Dribbble hosts are no slouch either. The have their homepage lined with images from designers around the globe. The spacing is beautiful, while it offers a multitude of designs all bying for attention, they are kept neatly in their containers.

White Space
WeRecycle.ch

The last thing you might think about a recycling company is how nice their website looks. WeRecycle has a delightful home page that loads in with a small recycling truck, and gives you a grand picture to look at with a bold title next to it. Yet even with the large entrance it doesn't feel like too much. There is a lot of white space in the top between the image and the heading. It balances out the large carnival picture and loud heading.

Repitition
Mcdonalds.com

It's no surprise that a multimillion corporation would have a nice website. What McDonalds excels at here though, is their remarkable consistency in style and color. Almost every interactive element in the website is highlighted bright yellow. This both lets you know it's ok to click on, and highlights the company's main color. There is also a sharp pop of red that periodically shows up, giving a great example of contrast.
